
Why I Built Prime Health 💚
Hi, I'm Anupam Saxena, the creator of Prime Health.
For over 9 years, I struggled with my weight.
Like many people, I believed the answer was simply to exercise more.
I walked over 10,000 steps almost every day.
I spent hours cycling.
I burned hundreds of calories through exercise.
Yet...my weight barely changed.
Then, in January 2026, after another conversation where I was told to "just do more running," I asked myself a simple question:
What does running actually do?
The answer was obvious.
It burns calories.
But I was already burning plenty.
So why wasn't I losing weight?
I decided to stop guessing.
I opened an AI assistant and explained everything honestly—including every cup of tea, every roti, every meal I ate.
The answer surprised me.
I wasn't failing because I wasn't exercising enough.
I was eating at maintenance calories.
That realization changed everything.
Instead of starving myself or giving up my favourite foods, I created simple rules:
Count calories honestly.
Keep regular home-cooked meals.
Limit extras instead of banning them.
Stay consistent.
Ignore normal day-to-day weight fluctuations.
On 23 January 2026, I weighed 79.10 kg.
By July 2026, I had lost nearly 10 kg, reaching around 69 kg—without extreme diets or impossible workouts.
Then I realized something.
If I had misunderstood weight loss for years, millions of other people probably had too.
So I decided to build the app I wish I had from the beginning.
That's how Prime Health was born.
My goal isn't just to help people count calories.
It's to help people understand how weight loss actually works, stay consistent, and avoid getting discouraged by normal daily scale fluctuations.
Everyone deserves the chance to reach their own Prime Form.
— Anupam Saxena











# Prime Health 💚

Prime Health is a simple and user-friendly calorie tracking and weight loss application designed to help people build healthier eating habits.

## Features
- 🍽️ Food calorie tracking
- 📊 Weight progress tracking
- 💧 Water intake tracking
- 🏃 Activity logging
- 🥗 Large food database with Indian foods
- 📈 Nutrition insights

Prime Health is currently in active development, with new foods and features being added regularly based on tester feedback.
